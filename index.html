Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28th 2026

Lingobear ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Lingobear app.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information such as your name, email address, or login details
  • Language learning activity such as conversations, lesson progress, and preferences
  • Device and usage information such as app interactions, crash logs, and analytics
  • Audio input if you use voice features in the app

How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the Lingobear experience
  • Personalize your language learning journey
  • Support conversational AI features
  • Monitor app performance and fix bugs
  • Communicate with you about updates or support requests

AI Conversations

Lingobear uses AI to power language conversations and learning experiences. Your messages may be processed by third-party AI service providers strictly for the purpose of generating responses and improving app functionality.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted service providers that help us operate the app, such as hosting, analytics, authentication, and AI infrastructure providers.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide the app, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your Choices

You may be able to:

  • Update or delete your account information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Disable certain permissions, such as microphone access, through your device settings

Children's Privacy

Lingobear is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
